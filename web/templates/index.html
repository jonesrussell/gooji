About Gooji

Gooji is a cultural preservation platform created specifically for the Sagamok Anishnawbek First Nation. Our mission is to empower community members to record, share, and preserve Ojibwe language and cultural knowledge through modern video technology.

Every video recorded helps ensure that the rich traditions, stories, and language of the Ojibwe people continue to thrive for generations to come.
